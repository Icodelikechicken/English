**Phân biệt các từ gần nghĩa, khác sắc thái**

annoyed vs. upset

- annoyed = bực mình vì ai đó/điều gì đó làm phiền => She was annoyed because the neighbors kept making loud noises.

- upset = buồn bã, tổn thương cảm xúc => I felt really upset when my friend ignored my messages.

ashamed vs. embarrassed

- embarrassed = ngượng ngùng (tình huống xã hội) => He felt embarrassed after forgetting his speech on stage.

- ashamed = xấu hổ vì làm sai/vi phạm đạo đức => She felt ashamed because she lied to her parents.

anxious vs. nervous

- nervous = lo, hồi hộp (trước sự kiện cụ thể) => I always feel nervous before taking an exam.

- anxious = lo âu, bất an (nghiêm trọng hơn, kéo dài) => He has been feeling anxious about his future recently.

## Áp dụng văn cảnh cụ thể

You know what, before I became an English teacher, I was a student just like you, wanting to score high in IELTS test. 
I spent months practicing before taking a mock test but I was totally dissapointed by the result. 
It was only 4.0. I was totally depressed and ashamed because all of my friends got better scores. 
But then suddenly I felt calm. I knew I needed to stop feeling miserable and start to work. Then I made a plan of studying everyday. 
I would not be satisfied until all the work is done. I no longer felt anxious, because I knew the right way to score high.

## Exercies

a. She suffered from severe depression after losing her job. => Sau tính cần danh => sự trầm cảm nặng

b. To everyone's amazement, the goal was disallowed. => Cần danh từ

c. Poverty is not a shame, but being ashamed of it is. => Danh từ và tính từ

d. The pay is pretty good, but you don't get much job satisfaction. => job satisfation: mức độ hài lòng công việc

e. The work is satisfactory but not outstanding. => đạt yêu cầu nhưng không nổi bật

f. His eyes were filled with anger knowing his daughter married such a childish man. => danh từ

g. She dreamed about a handsome young prince coming to rescue her from her misery. => danh từ: cảnh khốn cùng

h. Visually the house is very pleasing, but it's uncomfortable. => tính từ tả vật => thêm -ing

i. It was delightful news for me to learn that you have received a Doctor's Degree from Chicago University. => delighted cho người

j. Going into hospital can be very frightening for a child. => khiến ai đó sợ: frightening
 
k. The teacher's calmness during the storm reassured the children.

l. Her nervousness quickly disappeared once she was on stage and sang the first notes of the song.

m. It has taken a lot of courage to overcome my embarrassment and return here to apologize.
